# Product Context

## Why This Project Exists

Vidiony exists to provide a modern, user-friendly video sharing platform where users can:
- Upload and share their video content
- Discover and watch videos from other users
- Engage with content through a clean, intuitive interface

## Problems It Solves

1. **Content Creation**: Gives users a platform to share video content easily
2. **Discovery**: Provides a feed of videos to discover new content
3. **Playback**: Offers smooth, reliable video playback experience
4. **Accessibility**: Works across devices (desktop and mobile)

## User Experience Goals

- Clean, modern interface with intuitive navigation
- Fast video loading and playback
- Simple upload process for content creators
- Responsive design for all screen sizes
- Authentication for personalized experience

## Target Users

- Content creators who want to share videos
- Viewers who want to discover and watch video content
- General internet users looking for a video platform
